# Robotics Engineering Journey

> "Engineering is ultimately an act of stewardship—of knowledge, of technology, and of the world we choose to leave behind."

Welcome.

This repository documents my journey from Mechanical Engineering to Robotics Systems Engineering through disciplined learning, first-principles thinking, and consistent engineering practice.

It is not a collection of tutorials.

It is a living engineering notebook containing everything I learn, build, document, test, improve, and eventually research.

---

## Vision

I aspire to develop autonomous robotic systems capable of operating where human effort is dangerous, inefficient, or unsustainable.

My long-term research interests include:

- Sustainable Infrastructure Monitoring
- Renewable Energy Robotics
- Environmental Intelligence
- Carbon Intelligence & Optimization
- Autonomous Inspection Systems
- Field Robotics
- AI-powered Robotic Systems

Ultimately, my goal is to build robotic technologies that contribute to a cleaner, safer and more sustainable world.

---

## Engineering Philosophy

I believe that great engineering is built on understanding rather than memorization.

This repository follows five simple principles:

- Understand deeply.
- Build consistently.
- Document everything.
- Think in systems.
- Solve meaningful problems.
