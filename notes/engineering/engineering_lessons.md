## Lesson 007

---

## Lesson 008

Use organization to reduce friction.

Not to feel productive.

## Lesson 009

Understand Before Memorizing

Never memorize commands.

Understand:

- Why it exists
- What problem it solves
- How it works
- Where it is used

Only then memorize syntax.

---

## Lesson 010

Systems Thinking

Everything in engineering is a layered system.

Example

↓

SSH

↓

TCP

↓

IP

↓

Linux Kernel

↓

## Lesson 012

Debug Don't Guess

Use verbose output.

Investigate.

Read error messages.

Never randomly try commands from StackOverflow.
